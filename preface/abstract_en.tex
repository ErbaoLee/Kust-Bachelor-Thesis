\begin{abstract}
{\TNR
This paper presents the design and implementation of a mobile robot control system. By utilizing multi-sensor fusion and Bayesian filtering, the robot achieves robust localization and navigation in complex environments. The experimental results demonstrate the effectiveness of the proposed method.}\\[5mm]
{\TNRB \textbf{Key words:} Mobile Robot, Localization, Navigation, Bayesian Filter}
\end{abstract}
